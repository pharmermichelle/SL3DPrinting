How It's Made

1. Submit Your Design

Send us your STL, logo, sketch, or even just an idea. We'll help translate it into a printable design.

2. Prep & Test

We optimize files for strength, consistency, and print quality, then test before full production.

3. Production Run

Your order is printed in controlled batches to ensure repeatable, reliable results.

4. Delivery

Finished products are packaged and ready for events, teams, promotions, or distribution.
